Hypernatremia & Free Water Deficit Matrix

Outpatient and inpatient management tool for hypernatremia. Calculate the water deficit, choose appropriate hydration fluids, and treat underlying Diabetes Insipidus.

CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING: Overly rapid correction of chronic hypernatremia (>0.5 mEq/L/hour or >8–10 mEq/L in 24 hours) causes water influx into brain cells, leading to cerebral edema, seizures, coma, and death. Check serum sodium every 4 hours during active IV fluid titration.
