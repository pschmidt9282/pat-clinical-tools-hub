⚡ Antithyroid Starting Dose
Suggested Antithyroid Drug
Methimazole (Tapazole)
Suggested Starting Regimen
5 – 10 mg PO once daily
⚠️ Baseline monitoring required: Obtain Free T4, Total T3, TSH, liver function tests (LFTs), and complete blood count (CBC) before starting antithyroid drug therapy.

Hyperthyroidism & Antithyroid Therapy Matrix

Evidence-based point-of-care tool to initiate and monitor hyperthyroidism treatments. Select therapies matching pregnancy status, symptom severity, and manage agranulocytosis risk.

CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING (AGRANULOCYTOSIS): Antithyroid drugs (Methimazole, PTU) carry a rare but fatal risk of **agranulocytosis** (0.1–0.5%). **Instruct all patients to stop the drug immediately and seek an urgent CBC if they develop a fever, chills, sore throat, or mouth ulcers.** Routine white blood cell monitoring is NOT recommended due to the rapid onset of agranulocytosis.
