AFib Anticoagulant Selection

Interactive mapping of Warfarin and DOACs (Apixaban, Rivaroxaban, Dabigatran, Edoxaban) for stroke prevention in non-valvular Atrial Fibrillation. Compare dosing frequency, renal clearance, GI bleeding risks, Beers Criteria, food requirements, and reversal strategies.

Important Market Change: Andexanet alfa (Andexxa) was voluntarily withdrawn from the US market in late 2025 due to safety concerns. 4-factor PCC (25–50 U/kg) is now the primary reversal strategy for all Factor Xa inhibitors (Apixaban, Rivaroxaban, Edoxaban) in the US.
