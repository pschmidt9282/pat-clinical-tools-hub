Reflex Test Interpretation: Transient Elastography (FibroScan / VCTE)

For patients with intermediate FIB-4 (1.3 - 2.67), order FibroScan to measure Liver Stiffness Measurement (LSM in kPa):

LSM < 8.0 kPa
Low risk for advanced fibrosis. Return patient to primary care management. Repeat FIB-4 in 3 years.
LSM 8.0 – 12.0 kPa
Indeterminate / Moderate Fibrosis (F2-F3). Hepatology consultation recommended or ELF score test.
LSM > 12.0 kPa
High risk for advanced fibrosis / cirrhosis (F3-F4). Direct Hepatology referral required.
