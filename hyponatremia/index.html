Hyponatremia Selection & Correction Matrix

Interactive tool to select and titrate therapy for hypotonic hyponatremia. Triage by volume status, clinical severity, and ODS risk to prevent central pontine myelinolysis.

CRITICAL SAFETY WARNING: Rapid correction of chronic hyponatremia (>8–10 mEq/L in 24 hours or >4–6 mEq/L in patients with high-risk features) can lead to irreversible Osmotic Demyelination Syndrome (ODS). Monitor serum sodium every 2–4 hours during hypertonic saline therapy.
