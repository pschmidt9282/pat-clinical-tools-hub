Lipid-Lowering Therapy Selection

Interactive pharmacological sequencing tool for lipid management. Differentiate statin intensities, manage statin-induced myopathy and CYP3A4 drug-drug interactions, and structure evidence-based add-on therapy (Ezetimibe, PCSK9i, Bempedoic Acid, Inclisiran).

ACC/AHA Sequence Guidance: In patients with high risk or established ASCVD, titrate to **maximum tolerated statin** first. If LDL-C remains ≥70 mg/dL, add **Ezetimibe** (generic, low-cost) next. If still above target, escalate to a **PCSK9 inhibitor** (Evolocumab/Alirocumab) or **Bempedoic Acid** if statin-intolerant.
