Hypothyroidism Prescribing & Titration Matrix

Evidence-based clinical decision matrix for hypothyroidism replacement therapy. Calculate Levothyroxine dosing, resolve subclinical treatment dilemmas, and review thyroid drug monographs.

PRACTICE UPDATE (2026 ATA GUIDELINES): Individualize therapy targets. Avoid over-treating mild TSH elevations in the elderly (≥70 years), as excessive dosing increases the risk of atrial fibrillation, tachyarrhythmias, and osteoporosis. Confirmed diagnosis with repeat testing is recommended before starting therapy.
