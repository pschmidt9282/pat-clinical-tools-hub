OpenEvidence Sourced Guidelines & Citations

  1. AGA-ACG Clinical Practice Guideline on Pharmacological Management of Chronic Idiopathic Constipation: American Journal of Gastroenterology / Gastroenterology. 2023. Recommended first-line PEG, followed by secretagogues (Linaclotide, Plecanatide, Lubiprostone) or Prucalopride for refractory cases.
  2. AGA Clinical Practice Update on Opioid-Induced Constipation (OIC) Management: Gastroenterology. 2019. Recommends PAMORAs (e.g. Methylnaltrexone, Naloxegol) or Lubiprostone for patients failing standard OTC laxatives.
  3. Safety Warning on Secretagogues in Pediatrics: FDA Black Box Warning. Avoid use of Linaclotide and Plecanatide in children under 2 years of age due to risk of serious dehydration.
