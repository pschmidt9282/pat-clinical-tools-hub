Insomnia Medications — Clinical Comparison

Interactive pharmacological matrix for insomnia. Differentiate classes by sleep onset vs. sleep maintenance, Beers Criteria (safety in elderly ≥65), substance use history, and relative rankings from the 2022 Lancet network meta-analysis.

Geriatric Safety Warning: Benzodiazepines, Z-drugs, and sedating antihistamines carry a strong Beers Criteria warning in patients ≥65. They significantly increase the risk of cognitive impairment, delirium, falls, fractures, and motor vehicle accidents.
