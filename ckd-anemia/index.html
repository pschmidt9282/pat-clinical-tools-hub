CKD Anemia & Iron Repletion Selection

Interactive comparative matrix of Erythropoiesis-Stimulating Agents (ESAs), Hypoxia-Inducible Factor Prolyl Hydroxylase Inhibitors (HIF-PHIs), and Oral vs. IV Iron formulations. Sourced from KDIGO 2026 Clinical Practice Guidelines.

KDIGO 2026 First-Line Recommendations: ESAs remain the recommended first-line therapy over HIF-PHIs (Recommendation 3.1.1, Grade 2D) due to established long-term safety profiles. In the US, HIF-PHIs are restricted strictly to dialysis patients, with a boxed warning for thrombotic events.
