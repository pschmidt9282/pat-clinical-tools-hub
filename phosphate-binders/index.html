Phosphate Binders — CKD-MBD Management

Interactive decision support matrix comparing calcium-based and non-calcium phosphate binders. Evaluate agents based on calcium burden, vascular calcification risks, daily pill burden, and pleiotropic iron/lipid benefits.

Vascular Calcification Warning: In adults with CKD Stage 3–5D, the KDIGO guidelines recommend **restricting the use of calcium-based binders** in favor of non-calcium binders. Excess calcium contributes to arterial calcification, adynamic bone disease, and cardiovascular mortality.
